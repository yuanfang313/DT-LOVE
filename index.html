DT LOVE is a VR training system, which is short for Discrete Trial Learning Over Virtual Environments. It is aiming at assisting a research to evaluate the use of Virtual Reality (VR) to teach skills to children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD).

And as the designer and developer, I'd studied and explored the various promising features as well as limitations of VR by this project, and expect one day it can become a real product! :)

Now, I invite you to take a short tour into my VR project!
